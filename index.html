Executive Layer

Chief Influencer Officer (CIO)

  • Sets overall strategy for influencer growth and branding.
  • Makes high-level decisions regarding partnerships and deals.

AI Legal Advisor

  • Ensures all contracts and deals abide by legal standards.
  • Provides advice on intellectual property and usage rights.

Management Layer

Deal Negotiator Bot

  • Conducts negotiations with brands and sponsors.
  • Finalizes deal conditions, deliverables, and compensation.

Content Strategy Analyzer

  • Analyzes trends to develop content strategies.
  • Suggests content optimizations to improve engagement.

Brand Matching Algorithm

  • Matches influencers with potential brands based on fit and values.
  • Scores and ranks opportunities for best alignment.

Operations Layer

Scheduling Assistant Bot

  • Manages calendar, schedules posts, and appearances.
  • Coordinates deadlines for content delivery and promotions.

Analytics and Reporting Bot

  • Tracks campaign performance and audience growth.
  • Provides regular reports on key metrics and ROI.

Support Layer

Content Creation Suite

  • Provides tools for creating high-quality content.
  • Assists with video editing, thumbnail generation, etc.

Community Engagement Bot

  • Moderates comments and interacts with followers.
  • Conducts sentiment analysis to gauge audience reaction.

Integration Layer

API Connectors

  • Integrates with social platforms for seamless content distribution.
  • Connects with CRM systems and marketing tools.

Data Gateways

  • Handles data flow between internal systems and external services.
  • Ensures security and privacy compliance for data processing.

Learning and Adaptation Layer

AI Feedback Loop

  • Continuously learns from outcomes to improve negotiation strategies and content suggestions.
